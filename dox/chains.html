<private> destroy

Destroy is called by the end method and it is here that you should clean up after yourself. The difference between `destroy` and end is the `end` is the verb that you raise on an object to ask it to go away and `destroy` is where you actually do the work to clean up. Think of this as the counterpart of `constructor` yet not called automatically. This uses the `before` chain which means the last one defined in the first one destroyed
